Sales Performance Dashboard


Project Overview:

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance through monthly, quarterly, and yearly trend analysis, product performance evaluation, regional comparisons, and KPI monitoring.

Objectives:

1.Import and clean raw sales data
2.Analyze monthly, quarterly, and yearly sales trends
3.Identify top-selling and low-performing products
4.Compare sales across regions and categories
5.Create KPIs such as Total Revenue, Total Profit, and Total Orders
6.Build an interactive Power BI dashboard

Tools Used:

-Power BI
-Microsoft Excel

Files Included:

  Sales_Performance_Dashboard.pbix, 
   Sales_Performance_Dashboard_Report.pdf, 
   sales_dataset.csv,
   dashboard_screenshot.png
  
Dashboard Features:

> Monthly Sales Trend
> Quarterly Sales Trend
> Yearly Sales Trend
> Sales by Region
> Sales by Category
> Top 10 Products
> Bottom 10 Products
> Interactive Filters
